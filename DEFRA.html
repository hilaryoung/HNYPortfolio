100+ Journeys to One: Rebuilding DEFRA’s eHMI Platform
Oct 2023 - February 2024
— THE ASK

Overhaul DEFRA’s 20-year-old import reporting platform into a scalable, modern system.

The eHMI platform is an internal system used to manage & record plant & food imports into the UK.

— MY ROLE

Sole Designer

Billed at Band 7 rate

  • Define user journeys & UI design direction
  • Facilitated workshops with stakeholders & SMEs
  • Supported developers in delivery
— THE CHALLENGE
Nothing is standardised

  • Overly detailed 1:1 item & form relationship
  • For every new requirements, a new journeys
Users defaulted to manual paper forms
eHMI 2/10 NPS
— MY APPROACH
Breaking down a complex system


What this shows
Possible reporting journeys, 500+ mis-matched UI & 100+ ways to report imported products.



Why we did it
To break down complexity & understand how workflows were structured for different scenarios.

The complexity is artificial & the system underneath is simple!



Only One System, Not Many

All journeys use the same backend, APIs, and data model. Pathways don’t change system behaviour.
Shared Data Across All Flows

Every journey captures the same core data. Different forms, same information.
Variations Are Just Conditions

Differences (origin, rules, type) are handled through logic, not separate journeys.

While the system is structurally simple, usage varies by role


.
.



By understanding true system structure & needs of different roles,
we move towards a standardised experience that adapts dynamically.


— TO-BE JOURNEY



Single, standardised journey

Adaptable based on user role, product type, purpose, priority, and country rules.
Same starting point, ensuring consistency

Journey diverges dynamically and based on context, showing only what is relevant.
Simpler experience. Fewer Error. Faster Submission

One journey instead of 100+ fragmented flows
